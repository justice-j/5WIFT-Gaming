About 5WIFT GAMING

Welcome to 5WIFT Gaming! We are a professional, close-knit cross-platform call of duty clan for competitive and casual players, where the joys of cod can be shared through like-minded individuals looking for friends and a good laugh.

5WIFT Gaming can offer you:

  • Teammates for core,hardcore and warzone
  • An active discord community full of keen enthusiastic players
  • A chance to help you grow as a player
  • Resources for your to provide your own content and gameplays for others to see
